My Projects

  • Virtual Cinema Records A desktop website for movie recommendations. Users can search up any movie and rate it, like it, or dislike it. Based on those user preferences, VCR will recommend movies to the user on the homepage. Once the user finds a movie that interests them, they can easily click the link for where it is available for streaming. It is an all encompassing movie recommendation and catalogue site.
  • CarMate A mobile app for tracking maintenance on your car. Using the app, users can get notifications for when it is time to do any service on their vehicle, as well as track all their service history on all of their vehicles.
