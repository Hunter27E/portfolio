Projects

Virtual Cinema Records

For people who want to keep track of the movies they've seen and easily get recommendations for their weekend movie nights. Users can search any title, read its synopsis and cast, find movies similar to it, and see where they can stream the movie. Users with accounts can like, dislike, and rate movies they've seen and store them in their movie catalogue. They can then get personalized movie recommendations by genre, actor, and similar movies.

ServiceMate

A mobile app for tracking maintenance on your car. Using the app, users can get notifications for when it is time to do any service on their vehicle, as well as track all their service history on all of their vehicles.
