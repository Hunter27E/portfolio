About Me

Who Am I?

My name is Hunter Estrada and I am from Los Angeles, CA. I am a 3rd year student at UCSD majoring in computer science to become a UI designer. I am also an avid outdoorsman and sports fan.

The outdoors. Hiking, camping, bouldering, road-tripping. Desert, forest, mountains, beach. All of it. I love visiting National Parks, Joshua Tree and Mount Rainier being my favorites. The fresh air, the still quietness, the grand landforms - there's nothing better.

What Are My Goals?

I'm studying at UCSD as a computer science major to become a UI developer. Coming into college I had no idea what computer science even was until I took my first course freshman year. That class introduced me to something so new and expansive that I chose to pursue the major. Computer science is nothing like I had ever learned before, and that is why I pursue the career.
